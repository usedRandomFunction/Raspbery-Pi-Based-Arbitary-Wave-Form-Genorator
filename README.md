# Raspbery-Pi-Based-Arbitary-Wave-Form-Genorator

this should be set to private, but anyway

the goal is to make a Arbitary-Wave-Form-Genorator (AWG) using a pi3b and some 74HC595's


## pin out

| GPIO | Use |
|------|-----|
| 16   | Shift register latch
| 17   | Shift register clock
| 18   | Channel 1 LSB
| 19   | Channel 1 MSB
| 20   | Channel 2 LSB
| 21   | Channel 2 MSB
| 22   | Channel 3 LSB
| 23   | Channel 3 MSB
| 24   | Channel 4 LSB
| 25   | Channel 4 MSB
